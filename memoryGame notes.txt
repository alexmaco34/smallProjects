GAMEPLAY:
Game has different levels of difficulty.
The background of evry square is gray at first, the game picks a random color for every time square couple, once the player clicks
the square turns from gray to the color previously chosen from the program.
Every time a player finishes a level, the board resets and more squares are added to the game.

GAME OVER:
For a player to lose, the ammount of given moves have to be 0.
The ammount of moves is determined by the ammount of squares /2 +1

Once the player has lost, a popup appears in the middle of the screen with "Game Over" text
with a "restart" button
